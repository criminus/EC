Free-Range Eggs

Food Alliance Certified define free-range eggs as those that come from birds that do not live in cages and have access to natural daylight or a vegetation-covered outdoor area for at least 8 hours per day. Each bird must have at least 1.23 square feet of floor space and be able to nest, perch, and dust bathe.
We highly recommend buying free range eggs not only because they are healthier but also to help local farmers grow their business. More and more farmers are forced to sell the eggs in batches to big supermarkets and businesses to make a living.

We are offering free extra eggs to each new customers when they buy at least 20 eggs and will set up a delivery schedule from us for a weekly delivery.
