Welcome!

This is a simple rock paper scissors game created using HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
The game it's very simple. You must beat the computer at the game by choosing rock, paper or scissors.
The computer's choice is random.
Before you start, you must specify how many rounds you want to play. You can play 1 round minimum and 99 maximum.
At the game screen, you will see your score, computer's score and the ties.
The winner will be chosen after the maximum rounds are reached.


Please go ahead and enter how many rounds you want to play below and hit START!
